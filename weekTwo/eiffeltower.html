      Overview

      The Eiffel Tower is one of the world’s most iconic landmarks, a celebrated fixture in Paris, the City of Lights. The famously popular monument has the crowds you’d expect—and the wait times to match. Breeze past the entrance lines with these skip-the-line admission tickets, which include guided visits to viewing platforms on both the 2nd and 3rd stories.

      Read More
      What to Expect

      Meet your guide about five minutes away from the Eiffel Tower, at least 15 minutes before your ticket time. Then, breeze past the crowds with your group and guide and zip up the tower on a speedy and stress-free elevator ride.

      Your guide will be on-hand to give you a quick orientation of the tower and the city monuments spread out below. Get a bird’s-eye peek at classic Parisian sights such as the Louvre, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and Basilique du Sacré-Coeur.

      Continue with standard entrance to the summit, where you can also explore Gustave Eiffel’s private apartments for a fascinating look behind the scenes at the life of the 19th-century French engineer, or grab a glass or Champagne at the bar (own expense).
